Independent Riviera mobility companion

Azzuriva helps riders find direct Riviera bus routes faster.

Azzuriva is an independent entry point for a public route-search app focused on Riviera Trasporti timetable data. The app turns the official PDF timetable into a route-first experience for towns such as Imperia, Sanremo, Ventimiglia, Andora, Albenga, and nearby Riviera localities.

What the app provides

Direct route lookup

Search an origin and destination, then review direct departures by day type without manually scanning the full timetable PDF.

Official-source context

Results point back to the official Riviera Trasporti source so riders can verify service details before travelling.

Practical fallbacks

When a direct bus is not available, Azzuriva keeps useful local mobility context nearby, including verified taxi contacts where available.
